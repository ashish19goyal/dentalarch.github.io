Oral Hygiene!
By Dr. Anish Goyal Tips
 
Your oral health is not just important for a beautiful smile, but also for a healthy body. Few tips that can help maintain oral health.

1. Brush your teeth at least twice a day.

2. Floss every day.

3. Visit your dentist for a dental exam and cleaning once every 6 months, or as recommended.

4. Check your gums to make sure they are pink and the gum line hugs teeth tightly. Consult a dentist, if, your gums bleed during brushing.

5. Stop smoking. Smoking puts you at risk for oral cancer and infection.

Regular dental care means you'll be able to talk, smile and eat comfortably and live a healthy life.
